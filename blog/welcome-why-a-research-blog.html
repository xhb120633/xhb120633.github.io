Welcome: Why a Research Blog?

Draft template post | July 2025

I will use this space to share in-progress thoughts on cognitive science, LLM evaluation, and scientific workflows.

Posts can be long-form essays, short project updates, or reading notes. The goal is to keep ideas transparent, iterative, and useful for collaborators and readers with similar interests.

In upcoming posts, I plan to write about think-aloud methods, model evaluation strategies, and practical lessons from building human-centered AI workflows in cognitive science projects.
